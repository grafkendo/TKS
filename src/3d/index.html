Tackticus 3D — urban warfare

How to play

You play Red (3 AP/turn). Allies are passable — you can walk through each other.

Move = 1 AP (clear) / 2 AP (rubble or rough terrain) · Shoot = 1 AP · Range 1-2

Click a green hex to move; click a red target to fire.

Mechs auto-face their last move direction. Heavy pivots with on-screen buttons (1 AP) or ,/.. Fire arc: 3 hexes at range 1, 8 hexes (two rows) at range 2.

Shots through buildings deal half damage. Platforms give +1 damage (high ground).

Enemy types

● Grunt — 1 AP, 1 hex, 1 HP (common)
● Scout — 1 AP, 2 hex dash, 1 HP
● Armored — 1 AP, 1 hex, 2 HP, deflects dmg < 2 (mega-damage required) — rare

Orbital drop points deploy one fresh enemy each enemy turn (mostly grunts & scouts; armored rare).

Supply crates & items

Step onto a glowing supply crate, then click it to spend 1 AP and pull a random item.

Beware: ~25% of crates are trapped — booby explosion, EMP stun (skip 1 turn), or mimic ambush!

● Demo Charge (1 free per player) — stand on / next to a drop pad, 1 AP, pad gone forever.
● Tactical Nuke (rare crate drop) — 2 AP, range 3, 3-hex blast, 3 GIGA-dmg per hex, bypasses armor & friendly fire applies. Buildings absorb the warhead.

Buildings degrade through 4 stages: intact → bombed out → heavy rubble → rough terrain (walkable but 2 AP/step).

Click a glowing inventory slot to use a repair kit, drop a mine, or arm a nuke.

Win when the enemy team is all destroyed or immobilised.

Controls

Q/E rotate camera · ,/. turn unit · T top-down · Wheel zoom

Settings

Numbers above neighbor tiles show AP cost to step in (1 = clear, 2 = rubble / rough terrain).
