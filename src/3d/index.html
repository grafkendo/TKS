Tackticus 3D — urban warfare

You play Red; Blue is computer-controlled.

Each mech: 3 AP/turn. Move = 1 AP (clear) / 2 AP (rubble) · Shoot = 1 AP

Click a green hex to move (cost shown).

Click a red target to fire (range 1-2).

Shots through buildings deal half damage. Rubble = 1 shot.

From a platform looking down: +1 damage (high ground).

Win when the enemy team is all destroyed or immobilised.

Q/E rotate · T top-down · Wheel zoom

Devtools:
tackticus.applyApModifier('r1','blitz',+3)
tackticus.applyDamageModifier('r1','heavy',+1)
tackticus.immobilise('b1') · tackticus.release('b1')
tackticus.setController(2,'human') — disable AI
tackticus.endTurn() · tackticus.winner()
